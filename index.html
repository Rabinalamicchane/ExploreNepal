Discover our Nepal tours

Whether you’re looking for a Nepal Trekking Tour or a Nepal Discovery Holiday, our small group tours will show you the best that this spectacular country has to offer. Get off-grid and immerse yourself in the vast valleys of the Himalayas with our trekking holidays in Nepal. If walking the Annapurna Massif is on your bucket list, our experienced local tour leaders will lead you through rhododendron forests, across rocky landscapes and along snow-covered passes to complete this classic walking pilgrimage. Choose to stay in authentic family-run tea house lodges or opt for some home comforts in Ker and Downey Lodges. A visit to Nepal’s capital, Kathmandu, is also a highlight of Nepal. Navigate your way through a hubbub of fruit stalls, spinning potter wheels, gilded shrines, sacred cows and noisy rickshaws in the city’s lively bazaar.
